Tamir's Recipes

Hello, my name is Tamir and I go to Merivale High School. I created this website for a project designed to popularize and feature different culutral food by showing foods from five different countries. I wanted to be specific so I chose countries and not areas. For example, you could say mediterranean cuisine but each country in the area still has very different diets and traditional foods. I chose the Israeli, Russian, and Chinese cuisines. There are many more amazing, tasty and unique fares, that haven't been shown here.

To pick a cuisine you can hover over the names and see a sample of foods from the area. Then press on one of the boxes and it will transfer you to a different page with the cuisine you pressed on. In each page are breakfast, lunch/dinner, desserts, and side dishes that are native to that country with an explanation and recipe for each dish.

Enjoy!

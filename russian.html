Russian Cuisine

Tasty Meat pastries, delicious caviar sandwiches, or a red soup the russian cuisine has a very large and diverse menu.

breakfast

Blini

Blini are small russian pancakes usually 2-4 inches in diameter created in Russia in 1000 A.D. They were traditionally served with sour cream or salmon but now it served with anything. Caviar, condensed milk, nutella, etc.

Side Dishes

Olivier

Olivier is the best russian salad with eveything inside. Ham, eggs, potatoes, peas, carrots, pickes, and mayonnaise. It is personally one of my favourite salads invented in the 1860s. Lucien Olivier who created this salad never told anyone the exact recipe and there are some ingredients which are still secret in the original salad.

Here is a great recipe for olivier: Recipe

Side Dishes

caviar sandwich

An expensive but extremely delicious side dish is a caviar or, "ikra" in russian, sandwich. Usually served on white break with butter, margarine, or cream cheese and caviar on top. My preference is orange caviar and not black although both are good.

salatka szuba

salatka szuba or dressed herring also known as herring under a fur coat is a layered salad composed of a bottom layer of diced herring and then layers of root vegatables, potatoes, carrots, beets, onions. And mayonnaise

Lunch/Dinner

Pelmeni

Definitley my favourite food ever pelmeni or dumplins is a russian food created in the 14th century in Siberia. Pelemeni consist of thin pieces of dough wrapped around small pieces of meat. They are usually in the shape of a half moon and are served with sour cream or "smetana" in russian.

Here is a great recipe for pelmeni: Recipe

Lunch/Dinner

Vareniki

Very similar to pelmeni and also one of my favourite foods vareniki are the same thing as pelmeni with a different filling. While in pelmeni it is meat in vareniki it can be either potatoes or jam. The word vareniki comes from the Russian word "варенье", varen'ye meaning jam because of its filling. Varenikis are also served with sour cream.

Borscht is a beet soup created in Ukraine under the Russian rule in the 18th century Borscht is a very famous soup in eastern Europe and Northern Asia, in countries like: Ukraine, Russia, Belarus, Moldova, Poland, Romania, and lithuania.

Golubtsy

Golubtsy or a cabbage roll is a famous food in parts of Europe, Asia, and Africa. Golubtsy were invented in the 16th century in Turkey. Each country has its own type but basically, golubtsy are cooked cabbage leaves with some filling inside. In russian tradition, the filling is rice and meat.
